About

Visual storytelling to elevate and empower voices,
increasing empathy and motivating behavioral change

BIOSPHERE is a Bay Area-based freelance creative studio run by Chloe Peterson-Nafziger, focused on environmental and human rights storytelling.

My work translates complex environmental and social justice issues into accessible, emotionally resonant stories. My practice is rooted in expanding empathy, elevating underrepresented voices, and supporting meaningful behavioral and cultural change through visual storytelling.

I collaborate with environmental and educational nonprofits as well as mission-driven organizations across sectors. Based in San Francisco and working globally, I take on freelance projects in photography, videography, website design, portraiture, and event coverage.

If you're building work that needs to be seen and felt, I'd love to collaborate.
